About us

We believe in best, We believe in you.

Due to the high availability of learning resources and easy availability of internet, Students are getting confused day by day. We at TheTechIntern, Solves the confusion of students and make them feel the technology. We help our students to select the best technology and let them create a career in it.

We support students to explore from self and learn and quickly understand most difficult technical topics. We strongly believe no subject is complicated. It's all the matter of the Teacher.

We at TheTechIntern maintain highly qualified faculties with realtime experience. All our training cover full hands-on practice. We have a tie-up with different certification authorities So that certificates will help you to represent your knowledge.

Our Selection Process

We have a regress procedure to select students for our course. All the students have to give an entrance test before opting a specific direction. Once the student clear the entrance test, the student will get admission to our specialised courses. Student can also apply for a scholarship based on the result of the entrance test. Student can interact one-one with the faculty and clear their doubts during and after the training class. At the end of every training, We cover one hands-on project, and the student has to submit one project. Last, after submitting a project, the student has to go through the exit exam to receive an excellence certificate.
